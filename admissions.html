Admissions

Admissions to our institution are highly competitive, with a holistic approach to evaluating each applicant. We consider academic achievements, extracurricular involvement, personal statements, and letters of recommendation. Prospective students are encouraged to demonstrate their passion for learning, leadership qualities, and a commitment to community service. Our goal is to enroll a diverse and dynamic student body that will contribute to and benefit from the vibrant academic and social environment on campus.
